Shrimp Tacos

Description

This is a recipe for shrimp tacos. I make it sometimes and it turns my toes into caterpillars.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Tortillas
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salsa Verde or Something idk

Steps

  1. Take the shrimp out the damn freezer and put them hoes in some cold water for about 20 to 25 minutes.
  2. Once they're raw and wriggling, dry 'em off with a paper towel or maybe like an old sock or something. You're not gonna make them smell any stronger than they already are.
  3. Oil those little puppies up. I want 'em glistening.
  4. Give 'em a toss! A toss in spices and seasoning, that is.
  5. Put all that shit in a hot ass oven. 400 degrees will heat those suckers up such that they'll be singing. Leave 'em in that hellfire for about 7 minutes I guess.
  6. There's other stuff to do but basically just eat the sea creatures.
