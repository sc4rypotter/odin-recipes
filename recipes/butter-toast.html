Butter Toast

Description

This is a recipe for butter toast. Geidi Grimes ate it when she was with child.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Toast

Steps

  1. Get out a piece of bread. Just one, please.
  2. Get out a stick of butter. No less than one, please.
  3. Melt that butter till it looks like piss.
  4. Put the bread in the toaster till it's toasty toast.
  5. Now put the toast in the bowl of butter and let it soak all that dairy fat up.
  6. eat it like you're a pregnant woman with an exemplary diet.
